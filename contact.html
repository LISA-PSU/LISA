Opportunities for students and researchers to join our team

PhD Students

We are always looking for motivated PhD students with strong backgrounds in industrial engineering, computer science, data science, or related fields. Interested candidates should contact the lab director with their CV and research interests.

Learn More

MS Students

We offer research opportunities for MS students in the Department of Industrial and Manufacturing Engineering. Students interested in gaining research experience should contact the lab director or current faculty members.

Learn More

Undergraduate Research

We have limited positions for undergraduate students interested in research experience. These positions are typically available during the academic year and summer. Contact us for current openings.
