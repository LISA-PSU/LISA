About Our Lab

Exploring the frontiers of intelligent systems and analytics to solve complex problems

Our Mission

The Laboratory for Intelligent Systems and Analytics (LISA) is dedicated to advancing research in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and complex systems analysis with applications in manufacturing, healthcare, and supply chain management.

Located in the Leonhard Building at Penn State's University Park campus, our lab brings together faculty, researchers, and students to tackle challenging problems through innovative approaches and interdisciplinary collaboration.

We focus on developing cutting-edge methodologies and tools that can be applied to real-world problems, creating impact through both academic research and industry partnerships.

Research Areas

Our lab focuses on several key research areas at the intersection of data science, artificial intelligence, and complex systems

AI & ML in Manufacturing and Healthcare

Developing and applying advanced AI and machine learning techniques to solve complex problems in manufacturing processes and healthcare systems.

Complex Networks

Analyzing and modeling complex networked systems to understand their structure, dynamics, and emergent behaviors.

Smart Manufacturing

Advancing the state-of-the-art in manufacturing through data-driven approaches, IoT integration, and intelligent decision-making systems.

Supply Chain Resilience

Developing models and methodologies to enhance the resilience and efficiency of supply chain networks in the face of disruptions.
